Salary - AUS$53,900 per anum +Over time


Your employer will be based in Australia at one of their meat processing facilities located including Dinmore, Rockhampton, Beef City, Brooklyn, Riverina Beef, King Island and Townsville as well as others as and when they become available.

Processing times


    Arrangement of offer letter          4-8  weeks

    Visa Application                         6-12 weeks

    Total                                     10-20 weeks


What documents are required?


    1. CV

    2. IELTS

    3. Passport, birth certs

    4. Photos

    5. Educational certificates

    6. Work references and Medical reports

    7. Application forms

    8. Video

    9. Processing

    - Submit CV and Video. On the video we would like to see the candidate at workplace demonstrating their       technical skills. For example a boner should be showing his knife skills. Please refer to the example video.

    - The CV and Video will be assessed. Successful applicants will be invited to attend a trade assessment. They       will get an information booklet containing key information that they need to read beforehand.

    - A qualified Australian person, nominated by employer, will carry out trade assessment. Successful applicants will       attend the orientation program.

    - Fill in all the information and complete all tests.

    - Visa Application.

    - Visa Approval.

    - Go to Australia (tickets paid for by the employer).

    - Start work!
